# Insights & Findings

## Overview

This analysis explores transaction-level data to identify behavioral patterns that may indicate financial risk in a loan approval context.

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Multi-Location Activity

Customers transacting across multiple locations were identified as a notable segment.
This may indicate:

* Irregular transaction behavior
* Increased complexity in tracking financial activity

---

### 2. High-Value Transactions

Customers with large transaction values (above 10,000) represent:

* Higher financial exposure
* Potential lending risk

---

### 3. Transaction Frequency

Customers with a high number of transactions may:

* Represent high engagement
* Require deeper behavioral analysis depending on patterns

---

### 4. Combined Risk Indicators

Customers exhibiting both:

* Transactions in multiple locations
* High-value transactions

…can be considered higher-risk and flagged for further investigation.

---

## Business Implications

* Transaction-level data alone can provide meaningful risk signals
